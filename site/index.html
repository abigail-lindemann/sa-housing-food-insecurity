Housing & Food Insecurity Across San Antonio

A neighborhood-level analysis of Bexar County · CivicData SA

In San Antonio, the zip code you live in shapes your access to stable housing and affordable food as much as your income does. This analysis maps housing cost burden, overcrowding, and grocery access across all 1,139 census block groups in Bexar County — revealing where insecurity clusters, how it overlaps, and where existing services fall short.

About this project

This analysis was produced by CivicData SA using publicly available data from the U.S. Census Bureau (ACS 5-year estimates, 2019–2023), the USDA Economic Research Service, and the USDA SNAP retailer database. All data, code, and methodology are published openly on GitHub.
